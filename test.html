About Us

Over 20 years of excellence in thread dyeing and finishing.

Mominah Dyeing Services is a trusted name in high-quality sewing and embroidery threads. With deep technical expertise and a commitment to innovation, we serve top garment manufacturers across Pakistan.

Established in 1992, our facilities in Karachi produce over 30,000 cones daily, maintaining European standards like REACH and Oeko Tex Class 1.

  • Advanced dyeing formulas
  • Lubricated threads to reduce breakage
  • Eco-compliant production
  • Custom solutions for every client
